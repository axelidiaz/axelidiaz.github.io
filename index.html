About Me

Hello, I'm Axel, a Business Analyst at COCC with a background in data analytics, process automation, and contract management. I specialize in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights and designing automated workflows that streamline operations.

Before transitioning into data analysis, I built a 20-year career in foodservice operations and procurement, progressing from hands-on roles to leading teams and managing national accounts. This experience taught me how to identify inefficiencies, implement scalable solutions, and deliver measurable impact through data-driven decisions.

I thrive on learning new technologies, improving processes, and collaborating across teams to translate business challenges into analytical solutions that drive performance.
