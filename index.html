About Me

Greetings! I'm Axel, a seasoned professional with a rich 20-year background across various industries, predominantly in foodservice. From the dish machine to the spreadsheet, I've navigated through every facet of the industry. Currently, I serve as the Manager of Contract Services at InsideTrack, where I lead a team that administrates contracts for large restaurant chains and procurement teams.

From a young age, I've been drawn to technology, driven by an unwavering belief in my ability to self-teach. Fueled by insatiable curiosity and a thirst for knowledge, I thrive on the challenge of mastering new skills swiftly. I enjoy working both unsupervised and within teams, tackling big projects with enthusiasm. My expertise lies in process automation, dedicated to enhancing productivity at every turn. I take pride in my work and am results-driven, always aiming to achieve the highest standards of excellence.
